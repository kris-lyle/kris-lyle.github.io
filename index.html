FAQs

  • pause Can I start and stop whenever I want?
    Of course. There are no fees and you are free to start and stop your admin on-demand subscription whenever you would like. After all, it is “on-demand.”
  • hourglass_empty What happens if I need more hours?
    Extra hours that exceed your purchased cap for the month will be charged a la carte at the price you’re locked in at. Next month you can reconsider bumping up if that’s what makes sense.
  • timer How long will it take to complete a task?
    Usually we can knock out your requests within a few hours, but we guarantee a 24hr turn around time at the most. As you can expect it varies by scope of the task. Creating a dozen users will get done faster than uploading a new drawings or specs.
  • person_add_disabled How do I remove you from our Procore environment if we cancel our subscription?
    That’s easy. Just deactivate our user-account and we won’t be able to sign back in. We can’t deactivate ourself though; so you’ll have to do that. You can find instructions here.
  • person_add How do I reactivate my admin account if I decide to resume my subscription?
    It’s super simple. Just reverse the steps you took to remove me. You can find these instructions here.
